# YESDR Consortium Membership

The YESDR Consortium welcomes participation from organizations and individuals
interested in contributing to the development and adoption of the YesDR.

---

## Eligibility

Membership is open to:

- Academic and research institutions
- Startups and small enterprises
- Industry partners
- Independent researchers and educators

---

## Membership Categories

- *Organizational Members*: Universities, research centers, companies, and startups
- *Individual Members*: Faculty, students, researchers, and independent contributors
---

## Membership Fees (Academic Consortium – 2026 Launch)

To ensure sustainable development of the YESDR framework and ongoing technical support to member institutions, the Consortium operates on a modest academic membership model.

### Academic / Organizational Membership

- **Consortium Enrollment Fee (One-Time): ₹10,000**  
  Covers Consortium onboarding, initial framework access, starter lab documentation, and an introductory technical orientation session.

- **Annual Membership Fee: ₹15,000**  
  Supports continuous framework updates, faculty development workshops, student training programs, internship coordination, project guidance, collaborative research activities, and access to new YESDR modules.

*These fees are structured to remain academic-friendly while enabling long-term maintenance, documentation, and community support.*

Pricing is valid for early academic members and may be revised for future cohorts.

---

## Member Rights and Responsibilities

Members may:
- Participate in Study Groups and Working Groups
- Submit technical contributions and change requests
- Access draft specifications and reference materials

Members must:
- Comply with Consortium policies
- Contribute in a professional and collaborative manner
- Respect intellectual property and contribution rules

---

## How to Apply

1. Review Consortium [policies](policies.md)
2. Submit a membership application to info@yesdr-standard.org
3. Upon approval, join relevant groups and mailing lists
